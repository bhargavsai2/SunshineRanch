Explore the beauty of the nature with incredible sunrise and sunsets!

53 Acres of Sportsman Ranch and Equestrian Paradise with a custom home and stocked 3-acre pond. Beautifully appointed home features French Oak wood floors throughout the spacious open floorplan which is perfect for entertaining. Gourmet kitchen w double oven, gas cooktop, granite counters, breakfast bar and huge walk-in pantry. Master Suite with sitting area and spa-like master bath with high-end finishes. Sunroom along back with multiple sliding glass doors offers incredible views of Denton to the south and evening sunsets. 7 car garage, workshop and storm shelter. Just 10 minutes from restaurants, shopping and services off I-35 & 380 in City of Denton.


With so much to do right in your own backyard, you may never want to leave Sunshine Ranch! Explore the ranch's 53 private acres - you can swim in the pool, fish or kayak on the pond or drive 8 miles over to Lake Ray Roberts for a change of scenery. Scope out the marina or pursue one of the park's lakeside hiking trails. Offering opportunities for biking, horseback riding, swimming, boating, fishing and more, Ray Roberts Lake State Park has something for everyone in your crew to enjoy! If you're up for a day trip, the cities of Fort Worth and Dallas are both around 50 miles from your home base.

Sunshine Ranch is located in Denton county at 6345 Trietsch Road, Sanger, TX
